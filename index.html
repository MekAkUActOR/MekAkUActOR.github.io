Biography

   I am an undergraduate student majoring in Information Security at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Ping Yi and Jin Ma, and minoring in Japanese, advised by Dongyuan Kang. I work on memory system security, adversarial attack and defense method (deep learning), vehicle internet security and industrial control system security. Currently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • RowHammer mitigation method
  • Detecting adversarial examples by feedback-autoencoder reconstruction
  • Attack graph generation technique for V2X vehicle internet
